Manage your SIM cards.

With MyanSIM you have full control of your SIM card, no matter what mobile operator, all from one app and one phone.
With MyanSim You can:
1. Check your balance
2. Check and change your Tariff
3. Check and buy DATA packs
4. Check and change your Valued Added Services
5. Buy and manage Ring tones
6. Do a Balance Transfer
7. Top up
8. Use the Loan functions
9. Use Roaming features
10. Connect with the Mobile Operator.

All this is packaged in an elegant user interface for your convenience.
I hope you enjoy this free service.
Note: To change between Operators, simply do as you did before. Go to settings, Sim Card manager and select your prefered current SIM.
